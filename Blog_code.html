Moroccan Pottery

The Art of Pottery is one of Morocco’s renowned know-how. Morocco’s pottery and ceramics are one of the real treasures of the Kingdom with diverse traditional and modern colors and designs influenced by Amazigh and Islamic art and before them, absorbing Phoenician, Roman, and Byzantine designs. Artisans carry on centuries-old traditions in their designs that are passed on through families.
